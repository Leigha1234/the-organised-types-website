Website Design FAQ

What platforms do you build websites on?▼
We build on Wix, Shopify, Squarespace and WordPress depending on your needs, budget and required functionality.
How long does a website take to complete?▼
The timeline depends entirely on the package chosen. This estimate begins once we have all content (text, images, branding) from you:
  • Micro Website: 5-7 days
  • Starter Website: 1-2 weeks
  • Small Business Website: 2-3 weeks
  • Professional Website: 3-4 weeks
  • Premium Website: 4-6+ weeks depending on complexity
We offer a quick turnaround service if you are on a tight deadline—just add this on during checkout or give us a message!
What do I need to provide for my website project?▼
Once you book, you'll receive an onboarding pack including:
  • Content checklist
  • Copy collection document
  • Branding assets
  • Login access (if applicable)
  • Any required imagery
We make this process as simple as possible.
Do you offer website hosting or domains?▼
Yes — hosting and domains can be purchased via us, please let us know if required.
Can I edit my website after it's built?▼
Absolutely! All websites come with full client access. We also offer a Website Training Sessions for £49 per hour if you want a detailed walkthrough.
Do you offer ongoing support?▼
Yes. We offer Monthly Maintenance Packages from £49 per month which include updates, improvements and small edits.
Do you offer refunds for website services?▼
Because this is a customised service, refunds are not available once work has begun.

Branding FAQ

What's the difference between the Mini Brand Kit and the Brand Essential Kit?▼
The Mini Brand Kit includes a logo, colour palette and typography. The Brand Essential Kit includes everything above plus sub-logos, icons, patterns, direction sheets and expanded branding elements.
Can I request changes to my branding?▼
Yes — each branding package includes one or more revision rounds depending on the package purchased.
What files do I receive?▼
You'll receive PNG, JPG and SVG files, plus a branded PDF guide showing how to use your assets.
Can I use my branding on physical products?▼
Yes — once delivered, your branding is licensed for your own business use.

Business Coaching FAQ

How does a coaching session work?▼
Once purchased, you'll receive a link to book your session and complete a short pre-call questionnaire. During the session, we'll walk through your goals, challenges and strategy. Afterwards, you'll receive a personalised action plan.
What topics can we cover in coaching?▼
Anything related to your business, including:
  • Pricing & Offers
  • Systems & Operations
  • Branding & Marketing
  • Content & Productivity
  • Business planning & Etsy setup
Are coaching sessions refundable?▼
No — but you can reschedule with at least 24 hours notice.

Digital Products FAQ

I didn't receive my download — what do I do?▼

  • Your account download section

What software do your templates work with?▼
Our templates may use:
  • Canva
  • PDF readers (e.g., Adobe Acrobat)

If it's a Canva template, yes — fully editable. PDF downloads are not editable unless specifically stated.
Can I use your templates for client work?▼
Only if you've purchased a commercial licence add-on. Otherwise, they are for personal use only.
Do you offer refunds on digital products?▼
Due to automatic delivery, digital downloads are non-refundable once accessed.
