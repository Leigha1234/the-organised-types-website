Why we created The Organised Types

The Organised Types didn't start because we loved spreadsheets (though we do). It started because we saw too many brilliant business owners drowning in the "middle ground"—that space between having a great idea and actually having the time to enjoy it.

We believe that organisation isn't a personality trait you're born with; it's a skill you're supported in. We stand for intentionality over intensity. Our mission is to strip away the noise of "busy-ness" and replace it with systems that actually give you your life back.
