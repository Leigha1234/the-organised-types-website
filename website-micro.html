The Micro Website Process

Our goal is to get you online quickly and professionally.

  1. Phase 1: Discovery & Content We discuss your vision and send you a simple content checklist. You provide the text and images.
  2. Phase 2: Design & Build We custom design and build the single page on your chosen platform (e.g., Squarespace, Wix, or other simple builder).
  3. Phase 3: Review & Polish You check the live preview and submit feedback for the single revision round.
  4. Phase 4: Launch! We connect your domain, test all links/forms, and transfer ownership to you.
