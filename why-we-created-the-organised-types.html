Why We Created The Organised Types

(And What We Stand For)

Our "Why"

The Organised Types exists because we were tired of seeing capable people — women and men alike — blame themselves for systems that simply don’t support them.

We’ve watched people with talent, drive, and ambition struggle, not because they weren’t trying, but because the structures around them were confusing, unsympathetic, or downright exhausting. And worse, when they reach out for help, it often comes with judgment, pressure, or impossible expectations.

We knew there had to be a better way.

Our Approach is Different

We don’t believe the answer to overwhelm is “try harder.” Trying harder only burns you out faster. It reinforces the sense that if you can’t cope, you’re failing — when in reality, the system is what’s failing you.

At The Organised Types, we believe in three things instead:

1. Clarity Understanding what matters most, so you can focus on what moves you forward.
2. Structure Systems that actually work for real life, not just theory.
3. Space to think A pause to reflect, prioritise, and act without the weight of chaos.

This is how support should feel: practical, calm, and empowering. Not frantic or guilt-inducing.

Our Values in Practice

Everything we do is grounded in these principles:

People before profit Success isn’t just numbers; it’s about supporting real lives.
Honesty over hype No buzzwords, no false promises, just guidance you can trust.
Support without judgement Everyone’s journey looks different, and that’s okay.
Action without pressure Progress comes from small steps, not burnout.
Boundaries Protecting health and sanity so work and life coexist.

How this shows up in our work

These values shape everything we do, from free tools to coaching. Every resource is designed to help you:

  • Feel less overwhelmed
  • Understand your priorities
  • Take meaningful action
  • Protect your wellbeing

We always give space for reflection, so you can act with intention rather than react out of stress.
