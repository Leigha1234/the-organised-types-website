Why We Created The Organised Types (And What We Stand For)

The Organised Types exists because we were tired of seeing capable people — women and men alike — blame themselves for systems that simply don’t support them.

We’ve watched people with talent, drive, and ambition struggle, not because they weren’t trying, but because the structures around them were confusing, unsympathetic, or downright exhausting.

We knew there had to be a better way.

Our approach is different

We don’t believe the answer to overwhelm is “try harder.” Trying harder only burns you out faster. It reinforces the sense that if you can’t cope, you’re failing — when in reality, the system is what’s failing you.

  • Clarity – Understanding what matters most.
  • Structure – Systems that actually work for real life.
  • Space to think – A pause to reflect without the weight of chaos.

Our values in practice

  • People before profit Success is about supporting real lives.
  • Honesty over hype No buzzwords, just guidance you can trust.
  • Support without judgement Everyone’s journey looks different.
  • Action without pressure Progress comes from small, manageable steps.
