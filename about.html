The Organised Types: Our Story

We're Sam and Leigha — the mother-daughter duo behind The Organised Types.

Two creative, organised women who've always loved turning chaos into calm, ideas into plans, and problems into beautifully designed solutions.

The core philosophy of The Organised Types is simple: An organised business performs better.

But this business wasn't just a “let's start a company” moment. It grew out of real life — out of challenges, resilience, and the decision to build something together that genuinely helps people.

How We Started

The Organised Types began at a time when everything in life felt busy and uncertain. Sam had spent years helping businesses with systems, design and strategy, while Leigha was developing her own skills in tech, design and digital creativity. We were already helping people — friends, small business owners, clients — without ever naming it.

One day it clicked: What if we built something together? Something we wish we had when we started? Something that made business feel lighter, easier and more organised for everyone else?

We combined:

  • Sam's experience in strategy, business structure, design and organisation
  • Leigha's talent for modern aesthetics, tech integration and digital systems

…and The Organised Types was born.

A tiny idea turned into a full studio — branding, websites, business tools, templates, coaching, systems — everything we naturally gravitated toward anyway.

What makes this business special isn't just what we create. It's who we are together. We're a team that genuinely cares. We know what it feels like to juggle life, work, ideas, responsibilities, overwhelm, dreams and deadlines. And we wanted to build a space that gives people that feeling of **manageability** again.

What We Believe

Running a business shouldn't feel confusing or chaotic. We built The Organised Types to help people:

  • get clear
  • get organised
  • get confident
  • get moving again

Because when your business feels organised, your life opens up.
