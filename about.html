The Organised Types: Our Story

We're Sam and Leigha, the mother-daughter duo behind The Organised Types. We combined Sam's strategy & design experience with Leigha's tech and digital systems talent to create a studio that makes business feel lighter.

Why We Started

We didn't just want to build a business; we wanted to build something we wish we had when we started. We saw how easily chaos could consume creativity, and we wanted to turn that chaos into calm.

"Rock bottom wasn’t the end — it was my launchpad."

I sat in my car at 2 a.m., unable to breathe under the weight of a failing business. I’d hit rock bottom, dealing with a breakdown and perimenopause simultaneously. But in that darkness, I found the "Do Something Principle." Waiting for perfect timing wouldn't save me. I realized I had to take action, even when broken. That journey from the edge of burnout to building this studio is why I am so passionate about helping you regain your momentum.

How We Started

Once I embraced the idea that action breeds confidence, everything shifted. I owned my mistakes, pivoted, and started helping others structure their own chaos. We created The Organised Types to ensure no woman business owner has to feel like she’s facing that dark night alone.
