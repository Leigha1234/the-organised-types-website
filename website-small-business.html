Who Needs This Package?

This package is perfectly suited for small businesses, specialist consultants, and professionals who have detailed services, multiple offerings, or need to incorporate a simple blog or portfolio. If you're ready to look established, handle more detailed enquiries, and clearly showcase your different services, this package is your next step.

Core Features Included

4-5 Page Custom Build Includes Home, About, Services/Products, Contact, and either a Portfolio or Basic Blog page.
Lead Magnet Integration Set up of a simple pop-up or dedicated section for collecting email addresses.
Customisable Template Design Professional, premium theme selected and fully customised to your brand identity, colours, and fonts.
Basic Booking Integration Connection to your external booking system on the contact or services page.
Mid-Level SEO Optimisation Page titles, meta descriptions, and image alt tags optimised for key service search terms.

The Small Business Website Process

  1. Phase 1: Planning Call & Strategy Define your site map, conversion goals, and gather integration details (branding, booking links).
  2. Phase 2: Content Collation You submit all text content and images for the 4-5 pages.
  3. Phase 3: Design & Build We build out all pages, focusing on clear navigation and smooth user flow.
  4. Phase 4: Client Review (2 Rounds) You test functionality, review design, and provide necessary feedback for refinement.
  5. Phase 5: Launch & Training The site goes live, and the recorded training session is provided.
