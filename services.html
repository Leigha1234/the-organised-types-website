The Process

  1. **Consultation Call:** Discuss your goals, audience, style and required features.
  2. **Creative Direction:** We map your brand colours, fonts and visual identity for consistency.
  3. **Onboarding + Content Collection:** You'll receive a simple checklist and copy guide to supply what we need.
  4. **Design & Build:** We create your website's structure, layout and visuals.
  5. **Review & Revisions:** You receive 1-2 rounds of revisions (depending on package).
  6. **Website Launch:** Your site goes live — with testing, optimisation and full support.
  7. **30-Day Aftercare:** We're here for tweaks, help and guidance post-launch.
